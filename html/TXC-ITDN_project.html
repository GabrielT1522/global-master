Texas Center International Trade Data Navigator (TXC ITDN)

This web application accesses the US Census Bureau's Application Programming Interface (API) to retrieve and navigate international trade data. The Texas Center International Trade Data Navigator (ITDN) is being developed for the Texas Center for Border Economic and Enterprise Development located at Texas A&M International University. The Texas Center serves as the primary source for dependable and up-to-date data concerning the Texas-Mexico border. In addition to conventional data management, the center actively conducts research activities that enhance comprehension of the region. Its mission is to offer valuable insights, facilitate informed decision-making, and drive positive change in this vital and dynamic area.
This project is still under development
